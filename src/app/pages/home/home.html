Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?
Simply browse our course catalog, click on any course that interests you, and hit the "Enroll Now" button. You can sign up with your email or social account and get instant access.
Will I get a certificate after completing a course?
Yes! Once you complete all lessons and pass the final assessment, you'll receive an industry-recognized digital certificate that you can download or share on LinkedIn.
Are the courses self-paced or live?
We offer both! Self-paced courses allow you to learn at your own schedule, while live sessions give you real-time interaction with instructors and peers.
Can I access the courses on mobile or tablet?
Absolutely! Our platform is fully responsive and works seamlessly across all devices — phones, tablets, laptops, and desktops.
How can I get internship opportunities through this platform?
After completing relevant courses, you can apply to our partner company internship program directly from your student dashboard. We have 500+ partner companies actively recruiting from our pool.
